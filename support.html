Frequently Asked Questions

  • What do I get when I buy the game?
    Everything. All 6 chapters and every ending are included from the start. There are no in-app purchases, no ads, and no subscriptions — one purchase, the complete game.
  • Do I need an internet connection?
    No. The game works fully offline after download.
  • I bought the game before. How do I get it back on a new device?
    Sign in to the App Store with the same Apple ID you used to buy the game, then download it again from your purchase history — there is no extra charge. Your chapter progress also syncs through iCloud.
  • The game is not working properly. What should I do?
    Try closing the app completely and reopening it. If the issue persists, make sure you have the latest version installed, then contact us via email.
  • Can I replay the game with different choices?
    Yes! You can restart chapters to explore different story paths and discover all the endings.
  • How do I change the language?
    You can change the language in the game's Settings menu. The game supports 14 languages.

Pricing

Full Dead is a premium, one-time purchase. There are no in-app purchases, no advertisements, no subscriptions, and no recurring charges. Once you buy it, the complete game is yours forever.
